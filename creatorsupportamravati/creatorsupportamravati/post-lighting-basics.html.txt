    <h1>Studio Lighting 101 — Easy Setup for High-Quality Videos</h1>
    <p><strong>Three-point lighting:</strong> Key light (main), Fill light (soften shadows), Back/Rim light (separate subject from background). Use diffusers to avoid harsh shadows.</p>
    <p><strong>Softbox vs LED panels:</strong> Softboxes are great for soft flattering light; LED panels are compact and adjustable color temperature. Mix carefully for natural skin tones.</p>
    <p>For small creators: use a ring light + soft fill or window natural light with reflectors. For podcasting: soft front light and subtle rim light help separation on camera.</p>
