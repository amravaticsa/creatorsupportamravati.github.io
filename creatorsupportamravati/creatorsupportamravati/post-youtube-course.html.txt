    <p><strong>Intro:</strong> YouTube success merges craft, consistency and strategy. इस course में हम आपको step-by-step बताएँगे कि कैसे एक channel start करें, videos produce करें और growth strategy implement करें।</p>

    <h2>Niche & Research</h2>
    <p>Choose a focused niche, use YouTube search, Google Trends and competitor analysis. Identify high-intent queries and audience problems.</p>

    <h2>Scripting & Hook</h2>
    <p>Start with a strong hook, 10-second retention decide करता है video का fate. Use short bullets in script and mention CTAs strategically.</p>

    <h2>Shooting Essentials</h2>
    <p>Good audio, stable camera, and proper lighting beat fancy cameras. Use lavalier mic, 3-point lighting or softbox setup for clean visuals.</p>

    <h2>Editing & Retention</h2>
    <p>Edit tightly, use B-roll, text overlay and sound cues to maintain pace. Subtitles improve watch time and accessibility.</p>

    <h2>SEO & Publishing</h2>
    <p>Title contains primary keyword, description first 100 characters matter, use tags, chapters and playlist strategy. Publish consistently.</p>

    <h2>Monetization</h2>
    <p>YPP thresholds, sponsorships, affiliate, paid courses and memberships — diversify revenue streams.</p>
