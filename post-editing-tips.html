10 Quick Video Editing Tricks Every Creator Should Know

Speed up your editing workflow and improve video quality with practical tips.

  1. Use jump cuts to remove pauses and tighten pace.
  2. Audio leveling — normalize audio and use compression subtly.
  3. Color grade a LUT or basic corrections for consistent look.
  4. Subtitles for retention and accessibility.
  5. B-roll to explain and visually support your narration.
  6. Speed ramps for motion emphasis in short-form content.
  7. Proper export settings — H.264, correct bitrate and resolution.
  8. Thumbnail workflow — keep editable PSD or template for quick thumbnails.
  9. Use markers to organize cuts and audio cues.
  10. Backup all raw files and exports on cloud or SSD.
